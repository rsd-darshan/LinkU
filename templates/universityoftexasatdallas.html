Scholarship Award Amount Eligibility Criteria

Renewal Criteria: Based on specific award details provided upon acceptance
Comet Scholars Program Varies
  • For freshmen and transfer students with strong academic records and significant financial need

Eugene McDermott Scholars Program Comprehensive support including tuition, stipends, and enrichment activities
  • All majors and nationalities eligible

National Merit Scholars Program Generous financial support package
  • For National Merit Finalists listing UT Dallas as their first choice

Terry Scholars Program Comprehensive scholarship and support
  • Selection based on academic achievement, leadership, and financial need
  • Four-year cohort experience with mentorship and service opportunities
Top 10% Scholarship $2,500 per semester (up to 8 semesters)
  • For first-time freshmen in the top 10% of Texas high school graduating classes
  • Must maintain Texas residency
Renewal Criteria: Maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA each semester and 30 credit hours per year
